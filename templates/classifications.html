Classifications

What does the result of your URL mean?

Phishing

If your URL has a high probability of being classified as phishing, it may be trying to deceive you! Phishing URLs are those which have been part of phishing campaigns in the past by trying to gain sensitive information from a user for a malicious purpose.

Malware

If your URL has a high probability to be malware then our model determined your URL shows signs of hosting files that can harm your computer. Malware will often be software with malicious intent and has many forms like viruses and Trojans.

Benign

These are sites that are most likely to be safe! The higher the probability that a URL you analyzed is benign, the more likely it is to be safe.

Defacement

URLs classified as defacement are those which the model thinks might be malicious pages hosted or hidden by harmless sites! When a page is altered by techniques like SQL injections or watering-hole attacks, a user may experience adverse effects by visiting the page.
